Bubble Hunt Game

Welcome to the Bubble Pop Game! Follow the rules and instructions below to play:

Rules:

  • Aim of the game: Pop as many bubbles as you can before the game ends.
  • You lose if you collide with an enemy or let a bubble reach the top of the screen.

How to Play:

  1. Move your mouse to control the player character (the fish).
  2. Click and hold the mouse button to create a trail, helping you navigate through the bubbles.
  3. Avoid colliding with enemies and ensure that bubbles don't reach the top of the screen.
  4. Pop bubbles by moving the player character (fish) close to them.
  5. Each popped bubble increases your score.
  6. Try to achieve the highest score possible!
